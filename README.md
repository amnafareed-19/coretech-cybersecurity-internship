## Questions & Answers

### What is Cybersecurity?
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, networks, systems, applications and data from unauthorized access, attacks, theft or damage. It involves technologies, processes and practices that ensure the security of digital assets.

### What are the main types of Cyber Threats?
1. Malware
   - Malicious software such as viruses, worms, ransomware, and trojans.

2. Phishing
   - Fraudulent emails or messages designed to steal sensitive information.

3. Password Attacks
   - Attempts to gain access through password guessing or brute force attacks.

4. Denial of Service (DoS)
   - Attacks that overwhelm systems and make them unavailable.

5. Man-in-the-Middle Attacks
   - Attackers intercept communications between two parties.

6. Social Engineering
   - Manipulating people into revealing confidential information.

7. Insider Threats
   - Security risks originating from employees or authorized users.

### What is the CIA Triad?
The CIA Triad is a fundamental cybersecurity model consisting of:

1. Confidentiality
   - Ensuring data is accessible only to authorized individuals.

2. Integrity
   - Maintaining accuracy and preventing unauthorized modifications.

3. Availability
   - Ensuring systems and information remain accessible when needed.

### What is Ethical Hacking?
Ethical hacking is the authorized testing of computer systems, networks and applications to identify vulnerabilities before malicious hackers can exploit them. Ethical hackers help organizations improve their security posture.

### How can Cybersecurity help CoreTech Innovation?
Cybersecurity can help CoreTech Innovation by:
- Protecting the company data.
- Preventing cyber attacks and data breaches.
- Ensuring secure software development.
- Supporting business continuity.
- Meeting security and compliance requirements.
- Protecting intellectual property and digital assets.
